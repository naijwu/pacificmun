Introducing this year's

COMMITTEES

PacificMUN is pleased to offer sixteen unique committees that embody controversy and diplomacy. Our innovative committees are suitable for a wide range of delegates: beginner delegates may enjoy the diverse perspectives of a General Assembly, while veteran delegates may participate in exhilarating, intense debates within a smaller sphere.

Position papers are due February 16th at 11:59PM. It is highly recommended that delegates submit a position paper in order to be considered for an award. Delegates in advanced and crisis committees must submit a position paper, with the exception of Ad Hoc.

If you have any questions or concerns, please contact Xudi Lin, our USG Committees 1, via committees1@pacificmun.org, or Adam Mawji, our USG Committees 2, via committees2@pacificmun.org.

Registration Overview

Delegates are required to fill out the registration form below. If you are a member of a school delegation, then select your school on the form. If not, please select independent from the appropriate field. If you do not see your delegation on the list of schools, talk to your head delegate and/or sponsor teacher and confirm that they have registered your delegation.


Please also allow up to 48 hours for your school registration to be processed. Finally, any sponsor teachers wishing to stay at the Pinnacle Harbourfront during the conference should fill out the sponsor teacher registration form, also listed below.


Any further questions regarding registration can be answered by emailing Semeion Wong at delegateaffairs@pacificmun.org.

Rooming Overview

All PacificMUN delegate rooms will be single-gendered quad occupancy rooms. Sponsor teachers will be offered single rooms. Please note that if there is a case where the size of the male or female portion of a delegation is not divisible by four, delegates of other delegations will be placed in the room. If the Secretariat is not informed of any requests, then the Secretariat reserves the right to place independents or delegates of other schools to fill empty spaces.


Independent Delegates are placed in rooms with other independent delegates. Any requests for roommates will be taken at the same time as delegation rooming lists. During the conference, delegates are not permitted outside of their rooms after their curfew of 11:59PM each night. The Secretariat along with either the Head Delegate or Sponsor Teacher will handle all communication regarding rooming. The secretariat will ask for rooming lists in the months leading up to the conference.


Any questions or special requests regarding rooming can be directed to dg@pacificmun.org.
